Things & Derps — Chapter 2 Texture + Evil Thing Tile Spawn Patch

Install:
1. Extract this ZIP into the repository root.
2. Allow all matching files to be replaced.
3. Upload/commit the resulting folders with their paths intact.

Changes:
- Uses the original uploaded World 1B and Derpforest backgrounds.
- Uses the original Sakura Headsplitter, Spider Stump Gun, Melted Derptriangle,
  Homunculus Derpstar, and Rotting Derp artwork.
- White drawing canvases were made transparent for in-game use.
- Adds the original World 1B and Derpforest music files.
- Evil Things are removed from ordinary Derp wave spawning.
- Evil Things now materialize over time on enemy-side board tiles.
- They remain stationed on their tile and attack from there.
- Rarely, an occupied Evil Thing tile stacks again, up to x4.
- Stacks gain health and attack strength.
- Evil Thing textures are horizontally flipped and displayed with a red corruption filter.
